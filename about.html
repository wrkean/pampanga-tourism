About Pampanga Tourism

Promoting culture, community livelihoods, and responsible travel.

Our Mission

We showcase Pampanga's heritage, cuisine, and destinations while helping visitors travel responsibly and meaningfully.

This site is designed to help travelers discover where to go, what to experience, and how to support local communities.

What We Promote

Heritage

Preservation of churches, landmarks, and Kapampangan history.

Local Food

Recognition of culinary traditions and community food businesses.

Nature Tourism

Responsible access to wetlands, mountains, and outdoor sites.

Creative Industries

Support for crafts, lantern makers, artisans, and local talent.

Coordinate with Us

For updates, collaboration, or verified visitor information, contact the tourism desk.
