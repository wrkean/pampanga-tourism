3-Day Pampanga Itinerary

A balanced first-time plan covering food, culture, and nature.

Day1

City & Heritage Trail

Morning in San Fernando heritage sites, afternoon museum stops, and dinner in Angeles for classic Kapampangan dishes.

Day2

Clark & Culinary Route

Spend the day in Clark parks and attractions, then continue with a guided food trail for signature local specialties.

Day3

Nature Day

Choose either Mount Arayat for light outdoor activity or Candaba Wetlands for birdwatching and scenic views.
