Welcome to San Fernando

San Fernando is the capital of Pampanga — a city that blends heritage charm with modern vibrancy. Known worldwide for its Christmas Giant Lantern Festival, the city is also home to beautiful churches, museums, and a thriving food scene.

The city earned its nickname "City of Smiles" from its warm, welcoming residents and festive atmosphere that seems to shine brightest during the holiday season.

Top Attractions in San Fernando

Metropolitan Cathedral

The stunning cathedral at the heart of San Fernando, a landmark of faith and colonial architecture.

Giant Lantern Festival Site

The plaza and grounds where the annual Ligligan Parul competition dazzles thousands every December.

Learn More →

Museo ning Kapampangan

A museum dedicated to preserving the history, language, and culture of the Kapampangan people.

The San Fernando Public Market

No visit to San Fernando is complete without a stop at the bustling public market. Here you'll find fresh produce, local delicacies, and the best prices on Kapampangan pasalubong including tocino, longganisa, and tibok-tibok.

📍 Visitor Tips

  • Visit the market early morning for the freshest produce
  • Try the local kakanin (rice cakes) from market stalls
  • December is the best time to visit for the Giant Lantern Festival
  • The city is about 70 km north of Manila via NLEX
