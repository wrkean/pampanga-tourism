Clark Freeport Zone

World-class tourism, aviation & entertainment hub of Central Luzon

Former US Air Base

Clark — Gateway to Luzon

Once a major United States Air Force base, Clark has been transformed into one of the Philippines' premier tourism, business, and aviation zones. The Clark International Airport now connects Pampanga directly to destinations across Asia.

Today, Clark offers world-class golf courses, casinos, duty-free shopping, adventure parks, international hotels, and a wide array of dining options — all within a beautifully planned and spacious former military base.

What to Do in Clark

Golf Courses

Play on internationally rated fairways including the Mimosa Golf & Country Club and Clark Golf Club.

Resorts & Hotels

Luxury and mid-range accommodations with top-tier amenities in a serene, spacious environment.

Adventure Zone

Zip lines, ATV rentals, paintball, and other adventure activities within the freeport area.

📍 Practical Information

  • Clark is located in Angeles, Pampanga — about 80 km north of Manila
  • Access via NLEX/SCTEX (take the Clark exit)
  • Clark International Airport serves domestic and international flights
  • Bring valid ID for entry into the freeport zone
  • Duty-free shopping available for arriving international passengers
