Outdoor Adventure

Thrills, nature, and exploration — Pampanga delivers it all

Adventure Awaits

Beyond its food and festivals, Pampanga offers a surprising range of outdoor and adventure activities — from hiking volcanic peaks to speeding through lahar plains on ATVs.

Most Popular

ATV Lahar Adventure

Ride all-terrain vehicles through the otherworldly lahar fields of Mount Pinatubo — a unique landscape formed by the 1991 eruption. Available in Capas and Angeles areas.

Hiking

Hiking Mount Arayat

Trek the trails of this dormant volcano through lush rainforest to reach the peak and enjoy sweeping views of the Pampanga plains.

Learn More →
Thrill

Zip Lines & Rappelling

Adventure parks in and around Clark and Angeles offer zip lines, wall climbing, rappelling, and other adrenaline activities for all ages.

Leisure

Golf in Clark

Clark is home to several championship-caliber golf courses where you can play a round surrounded by the former base's wide, verdant landscape.

Nature

Wetland Boat Tours

Explore the Candaba Wetlands by bangka (outrigger boat), drifting through channels surrounded by water lilies and teeming birdlife.

Leisure

Resort Swimming

Numerous resorts throughout Pampanga offer day swimming packages with pools, picnic areas, and Kapampangan food — perfect for family outings.
